Moaz's Pokémon Awards

I proudly represent the First Edition, Third Generation of Moaz's Pokémon Awards.

The Awards are exclusive for the 3rd Generation (cause its my fav).
The First Edition contains 4 Categories:

  • Best Starter
  • The Cutest
  • Best Shiny (Non-Mythical/Legendary)
  • The Ugliest

So Now, Let's Begin The Ceremony!!!!

The Best Starter Pokémon: Mudkip

Do I really have to explain this choice??! He is the best by far, He is super effective against 3 of the gym leaders. + He is extremely cute. If you take care of him good enough you will get a Swampert, which is by far stronger than most Hoenn Pokémon, he can even compete against Pseudo-Legendary Pokémons such as Aggron.

The cutest Pokémon: Seedot

Man, I just melt looking at him. What adds to his cuteness level is how "tough" he is (get it, because he's a seed). The fact that he doesn't smile and evolves into a Dark-type? Now that is the definition of cute.

The Best Shiny (Non Mythical/Legendary) Pokémon: Beautifly

I'm totally biased. Beautifly was the one and only shiny I caught in Emerald. Seeing that purple Wurmple, I was extremely scared that it might evolve into a Cascoon and eventually a Dustox. I really hate that MF (he was one of the nominees for the next "award"). I was relieved when I saw that Silcoon and finally got a perfectly colored Beautifly. Man, oh man, is that shiny great. Now I crave shiny hunting.

The Ugliest Pokémon: Glalie

Hoenn had multiple ugly fellows, which made this award the hardest. From a Muk ripoff to a literal moon rock, they're truly ugly. But Glalie is on another level. He is so ugly that when Froslass was announced, people thought it was a glow-up, even though Froslass is still mighty ugly. I don't have a problem with Snorunt though; he's cool (literally).
